About Us

CUIJPER GIRLS PROJECT is a community Project which is serving girls and women in line of their rights and responsibility. It was founded on Feb 2010 and it is playing a role in fostering the community through; Reproductive/mental health mentorship, advocating for SGBV, Children rights sensitization, creating jobs opportunity through economic empowerment, referrals of school dropout girls to a friendly school, rehabilitation to the drugs addicts, Sensitizing the community on HIV/AIDS and other chronicle diseases, Organizing of peace talks forum to educate the community on peace keeping. talents and skills development, Offering feeding program to embrace zero hunger, C.G.P intend to be one and the best Project in the community which provides holistic and quality service to the community, with the slogan “WE CARE WE SHARE THE COMMUNITY WE WANT” and “ZERO CRY, HEROE SMILE”.
